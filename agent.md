## OpenSpec

- Use OpenSpec for planned and active implementation work.
- Put change-specific proposals, designs, tasks, and delta specs under `openspec/changes/<change-name>/`.
- Put stable accepted behavior under `openspec/specs/<capability>/spec.md`.
- Archive completed changes under `openspec/changes/archive/YYYY-MM-DD-<change-name>/`.
